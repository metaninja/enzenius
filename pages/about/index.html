About Us

WELCOME TO ENZENIUS EDUCATION CONSULTING SERVICES

Enzenius is a one-stop solution for all your international education & immigration formalities, ranging from decision making on where to study to arriving and settling down in your destination country like New Zealand, Australia, Canada, USA and Japan. Our principle is simple- your success is our success. Enzenius has been established by immigrants like you, who had the dream of obtaining world-class education and worked hard to achieve that and later on some of us decided to settle down in our adopted land. We understand the challenges of going abroad to study on your own, leaving your familiar surroundings for a unknown land for the betterment of your future and new opportunities. We have developed our services from the experience of people like you so that you can rest easy and focus on your study.

We have experienced teams located in New Zealand, Indonesia, Singapore and Bangladesh helping our guests with their overseas education and immigration needs. Our guests come primarily through word-of-mouth referrals from other satisfied guests who have moved overseas for higher education and work.

Our Mission

Our mission is to become #1 trusted higher education and immigration services provider in the markets we operate. Enzenius is built on strong values that empathies with its customers (i.e., guests), offering personalized services that understands the stress and worry associated with going abroad for higher education and the immigration process.

Our aim is:

  • To give students advice and guidance on the most relevant training and academic courses available in the New Zealand, Australia, UK, Canada and USA.
  • To be fully informed about our partner institutions so as to ensure students can make the best decisions on where to study in the New Zealand, Australia, UK, Canada and USA
  • To assist with VISA applications, clearly explaining all legal restrictions to students
  • To attend and hold student and representative fairs in the markets we operate
  • To arrange suitable accommodation for students when necessary
  • To provide advice and guidance about studying away from home in another country.
