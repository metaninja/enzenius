Enzenius (New Zealand)

Mohammed Mahin

Mahin is the New Zealand Representative and the founding member of Enzenius Ltd in Auckland, New Zealand. Mahin is a dedicated student enrollment professional who takes care of all student related issues that can arise in New Zealand. He ensures that our clients are well taken care of on their arrival in New Zealand. No other company can offer such personalized and professional service of Mahin. Mahin has completed Bachelor in Business Management from the UK and Graduate Diploma in Business Management, from New Zealand. He has extensive experience in managing affairs of international students in South Asia and South East Asian countries. He has professional contacts with many of the reputed institutions in New Zealand and Australia. He is based in New Zealand and fully dedicated to serving international students there.

Ricardo Deiana

Ricardo is our newest NZ representative and he’s also based in Auckland. Coming from Italy, Ricardo is our European marketing expert which also take care of South America; he can speak English, Italian and Spanish. His degree in Biology completed in Italy in 2006 makes him very knowledgeable about all the “behind the scene” of the Italian/European school and university systems. After few years spent between Australia and Italy, Ricardo has chosen to establish himself here in New Zealand and, although his extensive hospitality career, he decided to pursue his passion seeking a career in the IT industry. Right after his graduation in Information Technology level 7 obtained in 2015 here in Auckland, he became a full-time IT lecturer and got deeply involved in helping other international students successfully apply for their visas, study pathways, and jobs. Ricardo is now a sales and marketing manager for a well established IT company here in Auckland and he’s willing to share his knowledge and network of contacts with people planning to move or simply complete an experience here in the beautiful Aotearoa (New Zealand).
