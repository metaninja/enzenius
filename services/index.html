IELTS Exam Preparation

The International English Language Testing System, or IELTS™ (/ˈaɪ.ɛlts/) is an international standardized test of English language proficiency for non-native English language speakers. IELTS is accepted by most Australian, British, Canadian, Irish and New Zealand academic institutions, by over 3,000 academic institutions in the Unitsed States, and by various professional organizations across the world.

No minimum score is required to pass the test. An IELTS result or Test Report Form is isssued to all test takers with a score from "band 1" ("non-user") to "band 9" ("expert user") and each institution sets a different threshold. There is also a "band 0" score for those who did not attempt the test. Institutions are advised not to consider a report older than two years to be valid, unless the user proves that they have worked to maintain their level.

Enzenius is Bangladesh’s specialist IELTS test preparation centre, with the experienced teachers, proven courses and comprehensive support you need to ensure success. Your IELTS preparation course options from Enzenius include:

  • 40-hour IELTS preparation (max. 15 students)
  • Private IELTS preparation (any duration, any schedule)
  • Day Long IELTS Intensive Courses on Reading, Writing, Listening and Speaking
  • Day long IELTS workshops to improve your chances for high score.
  • IELTS practise tests & consultations (get test experience, estimate your current ability)
